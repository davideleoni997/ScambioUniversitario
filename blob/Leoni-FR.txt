The system shall provide the following filters :
-University
-City
-Subject
-Type of book.

The system shall be able to order search results by distance (closest to furthest) or by date (newest to oldest).

The system shall provide information to a specified user about other users reviews.

The system shall prevent the sign-up of the same user by comparing the e-mail address with the ones in the DB. A popup shall appear on the screen.

The system shall provide the user information about the article they are buying.
